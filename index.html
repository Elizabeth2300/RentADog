About Us

Dogs are everyone’s friend. That is why Christina McRae invented RentADog. It gives everyone the joy of having a dog or puppy, but temporarily. There is a wide range of dogs breeds we have. If there is one that you specifically want we can source the breed in one of our many local pop-up stores. The dogs are all trained and ready to be played with. We only have kid friendly breeds to ensure safety in all our customers. We provide dogs/puppies for parties, barbecues, and other events. Please consider borrowing a canine from RentADog, a way to have a dog without the full commitment.
